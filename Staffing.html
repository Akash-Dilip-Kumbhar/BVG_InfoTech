Our Services

Staffing Services

Permanent Staffing (FTE)
  • Hire staff for a permanent role with the organization as per requirement.
  • Employer Branding through recruitment.
Contract Staffing (Subcon)
  • Hire staff on Autoone’s payroll & deployed to customer location for a stipulated period then chances to release or convert on permanent role of client.
Contract to Hire (C2H)
  • Hire staff on permanent role after successful evaluation during contract period.
Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)
  • Recruitment process outsourcing
  • End to End recruitment services direct from customer location
Hire Train Deployment (HTD)
  • Hire 0-1 yrs of Experience.
  • Train them on specific skills & Technology
  • Deploy the final selects to company location on FTE 0r C2H method.

Recruitment Process

01
Position Assessment

Understand the requirement vacancy, company profile, culture, & ethics to source the right fit employee.

02
Advertise & Source

Advertising the vacancy & source the right candidates through job portals, professional networking sites, social media, headhunting & direct / headhunting, & direct / indirect references. Mainly focus is on Strategic Hiring, Niche hiring, Leadership hiring along with the senior level IT / Non-IT hiring.

03
Screen & Identify Candidates

Screening the candidate from the resume pool as per the requirement of the client & preliminary assessment through the 1st round the interview. We also have internal technical panel of ODC who does the pre- evaluation before presenting to the client.

04
Promoting Client & Position

Communicate about the company’s profile & vacancy of the potential candidates

05
Candidate Profile Submission

We follow maximum 24 hours TAT time to submit profiles.

06
Facilitate the client & candidate connection

Facilitate the client & candidate connection for the rounds of the interview.

07
Selection & Follow-up Process

Follow-up with the Clients regarding the selection / rejection report & communication to the candidates.

08
Onboarding

Follow-up with client & candidates till onboarding.
